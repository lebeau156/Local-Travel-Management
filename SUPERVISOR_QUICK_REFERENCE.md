## â“ Common Questions

**Q: How do I see ONLY my assigned inspectors?**  
A: The system automatically shows only your assigned inspectors in the Inspector dropdown.

**Q: Can I filter by multiple inspectors at once?**  
A: Not currently. Filter by inspector, then by another one separately. Or use State/Circuit to see multiple inspectors from a region.

**Q: The filters aren't showing!**  
A: Press Ctrl+Shift+R to hard refresh your browser.

**Q: Can I export filtered results?**  
A: Not directly, but you can click each voucher and download Excel individually. (Feature request: Bulk export)

**Q: How do I clear all filters?**  
A: Set each dropdown back to "All..." and click Apply Filters.
