## 2. Current Challenges: A Day in the Life

### üë∑ **For Inspectors:**
1. **Complex Form**: AD-616 has 15+ fields that are confusing
2. **Manual Calculations**: Inspectors calculate mileage using MapQuest or Google Maps separately
3. **Handwritten Forms**: Many inspectors fill forms by hand, scan, and email
4. **No Visibility**: After submission, inspectors don't know if their voucher was received, approved, or rejected
5. **Frequent Rejections**: Errors lead to resubmission cycles, delaying reimbursement

### üë©‚Äçüíº **For Supervisors (FLS/SCSI):**
1. **Email Overload**: 20-30 inspectors sending forms via email
2. **Manual Review**: Print every voucher and Excel travel log
3. **Error Checking**: Manually verify dates, mileage, calculations
4. **Inconsistent Formats**: Mix of handwritten, typed, and scanned documents
5. **Time-Consuming Approval**: Sign each form individually, scan, merge PDFs
6. **Communication Burden**: Constant reminders to submit forms on time

### üöó **For Fleet Managers:**
1. **Large PDF Files**: Receive 50+ page PDFs with mixed quality scans
2. **Difficult Navigation**: Hard to find specific vouchers in massive files
3. **No Data Analytics**: Can't easily track trends, total mileage, or budget
4. **Manual Record-Keeping**: Re-enter data into tracking spreadsheets

### üí∞ **Cost to USDA:**
- **Labor Cost**: 10+ hours per supervisor per month = **$3,000+/month** in wasted labor
- **Paper/Printing**: 500+ pages/month = **$200+/month** per circuit
- **Delayed Reimbursements**: Employee dissatisfaction and morale impact
- **Error Rework**: 30% of forms require resubmission

## 3. The Solution: Digital Transformation

### Vision Statement
> *"Empower USDA field inspectors with a simple, transparent, and paperless travel reimbursement system that respects their time and modernizes government operations."*

### Core Principles
1. **Simplicity First**: If it takes more than 3 clicks, it's too complex
2. **Mobile-Ready**: Field inspectors work on the road, not at desks
3. **Transparency**: Everyone can see status updates in real-time
4. **Automation**: Let technology handle calculations and routing
5. **Zero Paper**: Fully digital from start to finish

### System Overview
A web-based platform where:
- **Inspectors** log daily trips and generate monthly vouchers
- **Supervisors** review and approve with one click
- **Fleet Managers** oversee all vouchers district-wide
- **Administrators** manage users and system configuration
